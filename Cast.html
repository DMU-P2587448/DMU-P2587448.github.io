Cast

Leonardo DiCaprio

A well know actor that has played in movies like Titanic (1997), Aviator (2004) and Shutter Island (2010). Nominated for Oscars on multiple occasions for various roles. In the movie DiCaprio plays Cobb a specialist in extracting information and most hidden secrets from the minds of his unsuspecting victims. Due to his skill set and profession he is a very wanted man, not only by the police but also his former employers and/or victims.

Joseph Gordon-Levitt

He has played multiple roles over the years, he his mostly known for his appearance in Mysterious Skin (2004), Killshot (2008) and Extra Luxx (2010). In the movie Joseph Gordon-Levitt plays the role of Arthur, Cobb's friend as well as partner. He helps Cobb with extracting the information from their targets, and just like Cobb he is able to enter one's dream and travel through the subconsciousness of their unsuspecting victims in order to extract valuable information for the client.

Elliot Page

Elliot Page is know for her appereance in films like, X-Men: Last Stand (2006), Juno (2006) or Hard Candy (2005). She was also nominated for Oscar's after playing the main role in Juno (2006). In Inception Elliot Page is playing the role of Adrianne, architecture student who has no idea what will happen to her. After losing one of his accomplices in crime, Cobb addresses her and introduces her to the "world of inception". As an architect, she has the task of building / mapping the world of dreams in order to extract information from the target. Adrianne has no idea how dangerous Cobb's line of work is, especially when she's just learning this thieving trade.
