Synopsis

Cobb (Leonardo DiCaprio) has the unique ability to enter anyone’s dreams, when the mind is the most vulnerable, through the use of “inception” technology. He uses this ability to extract valuable secrets and ideas, all at the request of wealthy and influential people. Cobb and his companions in crime, Arthur (Joseph Gordon-Levitt) and Adriane (Elliot Page), face the greatest danger in their industry, the inability to distinguish dream from reality, while they forge through the layers of one of their target’s mind, a young heir who is to inherit a business empire from his dying father. Their employer is a powerful and influential man, who to make sure that the task is complete and that Cobb doesn’t deceive him accompanies him and his crew during the extraction of information form the young heir.
